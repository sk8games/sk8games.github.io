Baby Panda Care Game

Fullscreen

Children love to engage in all types of role-playing games, and imitating their parents is one of the most popular. Caring for a child is an experience they have gained themselves. Children are interested in changing roles and taking on some caregiving responsibilities. BabyBus gives children the opportunity to take care of another baby, an adorable panda cub. You will be surprised at how much your kids will enjoy caring for this newborn!
